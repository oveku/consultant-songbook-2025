# Get the future they want – Image Prompts for Video Clips

These prompts are written so you can paste them directly into an AI image generator to create stills for a video based on the song **«Get the Future De Vil» (Capgemini)**.

---

## Scene 1 – Naive beginning

**Prompt 1 – Young consultant enters the office**  
A young Norwegian consultant, early 20s, walking into a modern glass office building, holding a laptop and a folder with a freshly printed CV, wide-eyed and hopeful, warm morning light, corporate lobby with plants and minimalist furniture, cinematic, realistic, soft focus, high resolution.

**Prompt 2 – Slogans on the wall**  
Interior of a modern consulting office, white walls covered with large inspirational slogans like "People First" and "Shape Your Future", colleagues smiling in the background, but with a slightly ironic undertone in the scene, moody cinematic lighting, soft shadows.

---

## Scene 2 – Hidden pressure

**Prompt 3 – Behind the numbers**  
Close-up of a computer screen showing dashboards and spreadsheets with green, yellow and red KPI lights, reflections of a worried consultant in the screen, dark office background, dramatic lighting, realistic style.

**Prompt 4 – Townhall contrast**  
Large townhall meeting in an open-plan office, managers smiling on stage in front of a big screen with positive graphs, while some consultants in the audience look tired and skeptical, slightly desaturated colors, cinematic, realistic.

---

## Scene 3 – Bench and bureaucracy

**Prompt 5 – Consultant on the bench**  
A lonely consultant sitting at a desk with no project work, staring out the window, a calendar on the wall shows many months passing, stack of unused notebooks on the desk, muted colors, sense of waiting, realistic illustration.

**Prompt 6 – Endless online trainings**  
Consultant alone at home at night, tired, sitting in front of a laptop with an online training course open, coffee mugs and sticky notes around, window shows a dark city outside, cool blue lighting, cinematic.

---

## Scene 4 – Cities and offices

**Prompt 7 – Oslo remote leader**  
Teams call screen on a large monitor in an Oslo office, the leader’s camera is off with a generic avatar icon, while a small group of consultants sit in a meeting room looking uncertain, modern Scandinavian office interior, realistic.

**Prompt 8 – Train between cities**  
Consulting manager working on a laptop in a train between cities, blurred landscape outside the window, open PowerPoint and Excel on the screen, tired face illuminated by the computer light, cinematic travel shot.

---

## Scene 5 – Colleagues as family

**Prompt 9 – Laughing in the cafeteria**  
Group of diverse consultants laughing together in an office cafeteria, coffee cups and simple lunch food on the table, warm light, cozy atmosphere, strong sense of friendship and support, realistic photography style.

**Prompt 10 – Late-night support**  
Two consultants sitting side by side late at night in a quiet office, helping each other with work, empty desks around them, city lights outside, dramatic but warm lighting.

---

## Scene 6 – Reflection and doubt

**Prompt 11 – Consultant at the crossroads**  
Consultant standing at a street crossing in a Nordic city at dusk, holding a laptop bag, looking at two signs pointing in different directions labeled "Stay" and "Leave", soft rain, neon reflections on wet pavement, cinematic.

**Prompt 12 – Broken promise poster**  
Close-up of an office wall poster saying "Get the future you want" with parts of the text torn, revealing smaller text behind saying "they want", concept art style, symbolic, muted colors.

---

## Scene 7 – Advice to newcomers

**Prompt 13 – Knocking on the door**  
Young graduate standing outside a big consulting company building with glass facade, holding a CV, hesitating before entering, cloudy sky, dramatic but hopeful lighting.

**Prompt 14 – Questions on sticky notes**  
Desk covered in colorful sticky notes with handwritten questions like "Lønn?", "Benken?", "Leder til stede?", "Tid etter kl 17?", overhead view, sharp focus, modern office style.

---

## Scene 8 – Final reflection

**Prompt 15 – Consultants dancing between numbers**  
Abstract scene of silhouettes of consultants dancing between giant floating numbers, charts and corporate buzzwords, symbolizing the dance between dreams, budgets and bureaucracy, surreal, strong contrast, cinematic.

**Prompt 16 – Walking away at sunrise**  
Consultant walking away from a tall office building at sunrise, seen from behind, holding a laptop bag, city waking up, hopeful warm light, symbolizing a new beginning, realistic, high resolution.

---

You can mix and match these prompts, change style keywords (e.g. "watercolor illustration", "3D render", "anime style"), or add "Norwegian city" / specific city names (Oslo, Bergen, Stavanger, Trondheim, Sandefjord) to match the lyrics even tettere.
